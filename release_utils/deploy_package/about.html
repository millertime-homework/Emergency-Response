About:

These scenarios are intended to promote awareness and preparedness in students and faculty. The scenarios contain pointers and helpful tips for managing emergency scenarios which might be encountered on a campus of higher education.

This project was made possible by a grant from the Department of Education, Office of Safe and Healthy Students. Project leadership was provided by the Clark College Emergency Management Planning Committee.
